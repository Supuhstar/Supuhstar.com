From the perspective of Polaris?

The spirit of the child you met millennia ago has been your companion in the forest for awhile now.

The forest has changed, now completely affected by the humans and their cities. It isn't the forest you tended to for aeons.

You pass the torch to that newer spirit, the one who's lived amongst the humans. You retire into incarnation as a mortal, living out your days reincarnating as humans and animals living in the civilizations and the forest that joins them, now under the purview of that newer spirit.
