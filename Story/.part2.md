Many years later.

Child grew up, did her best. Never forgot the forest, returned to the sacred canyon annually, advocated in the village to pivot away from the new ways and back to the ways of the elders.

Eventually she becomes an elder, eventually the only one who advocates for harmony with the forest.

You eventually work with her to set up a pact with the village, where they bring you offerings and you spend extra effort maintaining the forest around their village. Addicted, they eventually ask you to also help their crops and livestock to be healthier and more bountiful, and you initially reject this but eventually begrudgingly agree in exchange for a blood sacrifice deep in the forest.

The humans become addicted to this way of life, even setting up a temple for you, with a fancy altar for the offerings.
